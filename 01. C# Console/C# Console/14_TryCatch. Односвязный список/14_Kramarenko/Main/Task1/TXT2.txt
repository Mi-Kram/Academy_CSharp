She slept too deeply: probably weakened over the years or
tired of digesting frogs. Hercules in the cradle, suffocating the serpent, behold
he, an embodied ancient myth. I did what obviously he did too:
quickly grabbed the snake by the neck. Yes, by the neck, and, of course, quite by accident.
In a word, a small miracle happened, which for a long time served as an object
soul-saving conversations in our family.
